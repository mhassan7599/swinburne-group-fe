About Us

WHO ARE WE

Swinburne Property Group is an independent commercial property advisory firm, specializing in commercial & retail tenant representation and advisory services. 

Our unique approach is to listen to our clients carefully, develop a suitable strategy along with a timeline and budget and work in close partnership to deliver a successful outcome.

Our business is based on trust, professionalism, integrity, data driven, and market knowledge honed over decades, and we strive to provide market leading independent researched based advice to optimize clients experience, reduce risks and maximise outcomes.

What we do

  • we act for tenants’ as their agents/representative – 100% dedicated to the tenants
  • we negotiate lease terms/HoA & leases on your behalf & provide advice
  • we source for new tenancy location based on your requirements & negotiate terms
  • we manage fitout projects on your behalf, when necessary
  • we act on the tenants’ behalf but we DO NOT charge the tenants*

In summary, we are agents for the tenants & tenants only. Whilst the traditional leasing agents act for the landlords, we are here to fight for you & will provide you the market leading deal.

Best part of our engagement is, we do not charge the tenants*!
